Choice experiment

The following game will last 8 minutes, beginning with a practice session of 5 trials.

You will be presented with two symbols to choose between. After choosing, you will either receive a reward of $0.50 or nothing from the chosen symbol.

IMPORTANT:
The different symbols are not equivalent in terms of outcome: with some you are more likely to win than with others.
However, no symbol makes you win on every trial.

- to choose the leftmost symbol, press the LEFT arrow key
- to choose the rightmost symbol, press the RIGHT arrow key

To have a chance to win, you MUST make a choice and press one of the two buttons.
Excluding the practice session, you will be allowed to keep the money you have won.

Press space to begin the practice trials.
